✨ Theme Identity
Modern • Minimal • Intelligent • Trust-Driven  

A clean, business-focused design language that reflects clarity, innovation, and structured thinking.  
The style feels premium, future-ready, and built for serious technology solutions.

---

🎨 Core Color Palette

⚪ Pure White
Hex: #FFFFFF  
Represents clarity, transparency, and simplicity.  
Used for clean backgrounds and spacious layouts.

⚫ Deep Black
Hex: #111111  
Symbolizes authority, strength, and technical confidence.  
Used for headlines and strong typography.

🔴 Signature Red (Primary Accent)
Hex: #E10600  
Alternative: #D90429  
Represents energy, action, and bold innovation.  
Used for buttons, highlights, and key focus elements.

---

🎨 Supporting Colors

🌫 Soft Grey
Hex: #F4F4F4  
Used for section backgrounds and subtle depth.

🌑 Dark Grey
Hex: #2B2B2B  
Used for subheadings and secondary text.

🔘 Neutral Grey
Hex: #BDBDBD  
Used for borders, dividers, and UI balance.
